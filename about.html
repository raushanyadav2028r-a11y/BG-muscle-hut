Building the Legacy

B.G MUSCLE HUT HISTORY

B.G MUSCLE HUT GYM was founded with a singular, high-impact mission: to establish a highly professional, modern, and results-focused fitness space in Bodh Gaya. We realized that local fitness enthusiasts faced a challenge in finding high-quality international-standard machines, structured workout charts, and certified scientific trainer guidance all under one roof.

Starting as a closely-knit strength club, B.G Muscle Hut quickly built an elite reputation for body transformations. Today, we house state-of-the-art strength and cardio training line-ups, helping college students, corporate professionals, and housewives reach their dream body shapes safely.
